叙利亚民主收复ISIS在叙最后据点 但安全威胁仍在

25k阅读·35分之前发布
ISIS
叙利亚
讲解
BBC

特朗普总统一直在解决的另一个问题与ISIS恐怖组织有关。

    President Trump has been addressing concerns the ISIS terrorist group. We'vetalked about this a lot since ISIS swept to power in 2014. Its name and its mission were thesame. ISIS stands for Islamic State in Iraq and Syria, something the group wanted. And itonce controlled a huge area of land in the region with a population of almost 8 millionpeople. Not anymore. On Saturday, the Syrian Democratic Forces, a military group supportedby the United States, said ISIS had been defeated in its last physical stronghold in Syria. In a formal ceremony to mark the terrorist group's defeat, the SDF paid tribute to thethousands of forces and fighters it lost in the battle but it said it would continue to fight againstany ISIS members who continued to operate and threaten the region. BEN WEDEMAN: In 2014, ISIS had a presence from the outskirts of Baghdad to Aleppo inWestern Syria at a time when it seemed that they were unstoppable, sending the armies of Iraqand other parts of Syria militias running before them. Now they have disappeared as aterritorial entity. They are no more. So this is a massive, sort of, turn of events when youconsider the situation just a few years ago. But I think that enthusiasm, even in this part ofthe world, of the region has been tempered by the realization that they still remain a threat asa terrorist insurgency. They are still frequent attacks by ISIS sleeper cells in this part of Syria and also in many partof Iraq as well. And of course, another problem is that if the regional government here or thegovernment in Iraq don't do a better job at reconstruction, at reviving the local economies, you could have a situation developing whereby discontent feeds into the ideology of ISIS, and you could have ISIS come back perhaps under a different name, in a different form. Butit's not just the question of defeating an organization like that militarily. They have to bedefeated ideologically and politically. And it’s not all together clear if that can be achieved underthe current circumstances.
    特朗普总统一直在解决的另一个问题与ISIS恐怖组织有关。自2014年ISIS崛起以来，我们进行了许多相关报道。这个组织的名称与任务一致。ISIS全称为“伊拉克和大叙利亚伊斯兰国”，这代表着该组织的目标。ISIS曾控制了这一地区的大片领土，所控领土上有近800万人口。但是这种情况已不复存在。周六，美国支持的军事组织“叙利亚民主力量”表示，ISIS在叙利亚的最后一块物理据点已被收复。 在纪念恐怖组织ISIS被击败的正式仪式上，叙利亚民主力量悼念了其在战役中死亡的数千名士兵和武装人员，同时表示他们会坚持对抗仍在这一地区活动并构成威胁的ISIS成员。 本·魏德曼：2014年，ISIS控制的地区从巴格拉郊区延伸至叙利亚西部的阿勒颇，当时ISIS看似无法阻挡，伊拉克士兵及叙利亚部分地区的民兵因无力抵抗而逃跑。但是，作为地域实体的ISIS已经消失。他们已经不复存在。想想几年前的情况，可以说这是形势的重大转变。但是我认为这种热情，即使是世界这一地区的热情，也因为意识到ISIS的恐怖叛乱活动依然构成威胁而被削弱。 叙利亚这一地区以及伊拉克的许多地区仍频繁遭受ISIS潜伏者制造的袭击。当然，另一个问题是，如果叙利亚地方政府和伊拉克政府在重建和恢复地方经济上表现不佳，那不满情绪可能会演变为ISIS意识形态，或许ISIS会以另一个名字和另一种形式卷土重来。问题是，不仅仅要在军事上击败这种恐怖组织。还要在意识形态和政治上击败这样的恐怖组织。而在目前的形势下，并不清楚这一目标是否能实现。
